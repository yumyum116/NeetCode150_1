## Score Of a String - Design Notes

### Problem Summary
- Found a space complexity issue due to redundant list.

### Chosen Approach
- Iteration

### Why Iteration?
- The simplest and only one approach.

### Strengths
- fastest : $`O(n)`$ 

### Limitations
- space consuming : $`O(n^2)`$

### Alternatives Considered
- Nothing
      
### Design Philosophy
- Chose the simplest way
