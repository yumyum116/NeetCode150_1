## Unique Email Addresses - Design Notes

### Problem Summary
- Extra memory is used especially for non-used variables.

### Chosen Approach
- Built-In Function

### Why String Parsing?
- Understandable and simple approach for beginners.

### Strengths
- Understandable and simple

### Limitations
- consume more memory rather than another approach and low resistance in malformed emails.

### Alternatives Considered
- Iteration  : High customizability and improving performance in specific cases, but potentially introducing more rooms for bug
      
### Design Philosophy
- Chose the simple approach
