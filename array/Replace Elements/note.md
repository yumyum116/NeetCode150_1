## Replace Elements - Design Notes

### Problem Summary
- Found a time consuming issue when compared with other approaches.

### Chosen Approach
- Brute-Force

### Why Brute-Force?
- The intensive and understandable approach for beginners.

### Strengths
- Intensive and understandable

### Limitations
- time consuming : $`O(n^2)`$

### Alternatives Considered
- Suffix Max     : efficient both in time complexitiy %`O(n)`$ and space complexity $`O(1)`$
    
### Design Philosophy
- Chose the intensive approach
