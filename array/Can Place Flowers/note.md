## Can Place Flowers - Design Notes

### Problem Summary
- Time consuming

### Chosen Approach
- Linear scan

### Why linear scan?
- The intensive and understandable approach for beginners.

### Strengths
- Fast enough $`O(n)`$ and no extra memory $`O(1)`$ 

### Limitations
- time consuming

### Alternatives Considered
- Iteration - I   : readable and high stability, but high space complexity $`O(n)`$
- Iteration - II  : fastest, but low readablity and potintially cause bugs
    
### Design Philosophy
- Chose the intensive approach
